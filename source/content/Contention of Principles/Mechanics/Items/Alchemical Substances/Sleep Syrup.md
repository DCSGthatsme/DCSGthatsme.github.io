## Effect
Moderate<br>A sticky liquid that, when ingested, causes immediate sleep if the target does not pass a [[Might]] test. The target stays asleep for an hour or until suffering any damage. If the test is passed, the person is instead dazed, with the effect that they are limited to one action on their turns and cannot use any active abilities for an hour.
